21. describe how the size of the population of a country will change as it passes through stages 4 of the demographic transition!​


Jawaban:

Penjelasan:

Stage 4 of the demographic transition is characterized by low birth rates and low death rates, resulting in a stable or slowly growing population. Here is a description of how the size of the population of a country will change as it passes through Stage 4:

1. High population: At the beginning of Stage 4, the country would have a relatively high population. This population is a result of previous high birth rates and declining death rates.

2. Decline in birth rates: During Stage 4, birth rates decrease significantly. Factors such as improved access to education, increased urbanization, higher living standards, and the availability of effective contraception contribute to lower fertility rates. As a result, the population growth rate slows down.

3. Low death rates: In Stage 4, the country also experiences low death rates due to advancements in healthcare, better sanitation, access to clean water, and improved living conditions. These factors contribute to increased life expectancy and reduced mortality rates.

4. Stable or slow population growth: As birth rates and death rates reach a balance, the population growth rate becomes stable or experiences minimal growth. The population size may continue to increase, but at a much slower pace compared to previous stages.

5. Aging population: With low birth rates and increased life expectancy, the population composition shifts towards an older population. The proportion of older individuals (above a certain age, often 65 or 70) increases, leading to demographic aging. This can pose challenges related to healthcare, pensions, and social support systems.

6. Population stability: In Stage 4, the population tends to stabilize over time, with fluctuations primarily driven by external factors such as immigration or emigration. The size of the population remains relatively constant, with a smaller proportion of young individuals compared to older age groups.

It's important to note that the duration and specific characteristics of Stage 4 may vary across countries. However, in general, Stage 4 represents a demographic shift towards lower population growth and a more balanced age structure.

30. buat lah karya tulis ilmiah yg baik dan benar ........ tenang Unilinear theories of evolution,universal theories of evolution,multilined theories of evolution.mohon bantuannya kak​


Jawaban:

Unlinear, Universal dan Multilined Theories of Evolution merupakan pengelompokan teori-teori evolusi yang berkenaan dengan perubahan sosiologi manusia dalam jangka waktu yang lama (evolusi). Masing-masing teori ini mengandung pengertian yang berbeda. Berikut penjelasannya lengkap dengan contoh masing-masing.

UNILINEAR THEORIES OF EVOLUTION

Teori ini menyatakan bahwa perubahan yang dialami manusia melalui tahapan-tahapan tertentu yang linear (lurus), dari yang sederhana menuju tahapan kompleks hingga sempurna.

Contoh masyarakat yang pada mulanya primitif di Eropa berubah menjadi masyarakat Industri yang modern.

UNIVERSAL THEORIES OF EVOLUTION

Teori ini menyatakan bahwa perubahan dalam masyarakat merupakan perkembangan yang tak perlu selalu melalui tahapan tertentu yang sifatnya tetap.

Contohnya masyarakat agricultural yang berubah menjadi masyarakat merkantilisme.

MULTILINED THEORIES OF EVOLUTION

Teori ini menyatakan bahwa perubahan atau evolusi dalam masyarakat ditekankan hanya pada penelitian atas tahap-tahap perkembangan tertentu saja.

Contoh teori ini adalah misalnya penelitian terhadap perubahan sistem mata pencaharian yang ada pada masyarakat, yang awalnya hanya sistem berburu menju ke sistem pertanian

37. Identify two stages of business activity ?


Jawaban:

Business activities are those which are concerned with seeking to meet the needs of customers by providing a product or service that they require. There are various stages in the production of finished goods, so business activity typically involves adding value to resources such as raw materials, to make them more desirable to the end user. Of course, one of the biggest value added is branding. For example, a well known brand like Coca Cola or McDonald’s adds a lot of value to the products sold by these companies because the brand is instantly recognisable.

A company like Shell, which is involved in oil and gas exploration, will extract raw materials from deposits of oil and natural gas, which may have been under the ground for millions of years. These deposits are then brought from under the ground by drilling operations. The oil and gas is then refined to make petrol and a range of other products. Shell adds its own special ingredients to create petroleum products, which are specially tailored to meet the needs of modern engines so that they achieve the highest levels of performance whilst, at the same time, minimising their impact on the environment.

Value added

A company like Argos adds value to the products that it retails by providing them in a convenient way for customers. Customers are able to browse catalogues at home (either online or in paper-based form) and then order the goods they want from their nearest Argos store. Argos therefore provides an excellent retail service for customers, enabling them to benefit from a range of value added provided by the store.

The term value added relates to the thousand and one ways in which modern businesses make the goods and services they provide customer friendly. These organisations research customer requirements in order to create the product and service benefits that their customers are looking for.

Business activity takes place at three stages of production: Primary activity involves extracting the gifts of nature (e.g. drilling oil from the ground); Secondary activity involves manufacturing finished goods (e.g. refining oil and gas); Tertiary activity involves providing services such as making finished goods and services available to the end customer.

•Pengenalan Masalah (Need Recognition)

•Pencarian Informasi (Information Search)

•Evaluasi Alternatif (Evaluation of Alternatives)

•Keputusan untuk membeli (Keputusan Pembelian)

•Perilaku Pasca Pembelian (Perilaku Pasca Pembelian)

Dari gambar diatas disarankan bahwa konsumen akan melewati semua tahap tersebut untuk setiap kali melakukan pembelian yang akan dipertimbangkan. Tetapi seorang pembeli dapat melewati semua tahap keputusan pembelian tersebut dengan cepat ataupun lambat. Dalam pembelian yang secara rutin, konsumenbiasanya melewati beberapa tahap keputusan pembelian yang pada sifat dari konsumen, produk dan situasi pembelian. Seperti, seorang konsumenmembeli merek pasta gigi akan mengenali kebutuhannya dan langsung menuju ke keputusan pembelian dengan melewati pencarian informasi dan evaluasi alternatif. Tetapi, gambar keputusan pembelian yang diatas biasanya digunakanuntuk mempertimbangakan masalah yang ada jika konsumen menghadapi situasi produk yang baru dan kompleks (rumit).
