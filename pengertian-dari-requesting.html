34. what is difference between offering, requesting , and invitation???


What is the difference between offering, requesting, and invitation?JawabanPembahasan

Offering berarti menawarkan. Contoh ungkapan-ungkapan offering antara lain seperti:

Would you like some tea? (Menawarkan minuman teh)May I help you? (Menawarkan bantuan)Shall I carry the suitcase for you? (Menawarkan bantuan membawa koper)Why don't you wear my shoes to go with that dress? (Menawarkan untuk meminjamkan sepatu)I can lend you $5 and you can pay it back next week. (Menawarkan untuk meminjamkan uang)Ms. Lee offered to look after the children while we go out to attend the exhibition. (Menawarkan bantuan menjaga anak-anak)Let me take your coat. Just find me when you're leaving. (Menawarkan bantuan menyimpankan mantel)

Requesting berarti meminta. Contoh ungkapan-ungkapan requesting antara lain seperti:

May I have some water? I'm parched. (Bolehkah saya meminta sedikit air? Saya kehausan.)I would like to exchange this red towel for the blue one. (Saya ingin menukar handuk merah ini dengan yang biru itu.)Could you inform the manager that I would like to see him? Thanks. (Meminta untuk bertemu dengan manajer.)Please leave your shoes outside the hall. (Meminta dengan cara halus untuk melepaskan sepatu)Send me the spare parts immediately. (Meminta untuk segera mengirimkan barang.)

Sedangkan invitation berarti ajakan. Contoh ungkapan-ungkapan ajakan:

Would you like to go to the cinema with me? (Mengajak pergi menonton.)We would be honored if you could come over and have dinner with us this weekend. (Ajakan makan malam bersama)You have been invited to enjoy an art exhibition on Sunday. (Contoh kalimat dalam kartu undangan formal.)How would you like to join us on an excursion to the beach this Saturday? (Ajakan bertamasya ke pantai)
