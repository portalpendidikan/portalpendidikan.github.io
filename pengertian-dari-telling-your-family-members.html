Jawaban:

Seri tutorial artikel bahasa Inggris kali ini membahas mengenai kosa kata atau vocabulary. Agar memudahkan pembelajaran, tiap vocabulary yang dipelajari secara khusus pada tiap contoh kalimatnya akan saya garisbawahi, mulai dari artikel ini.

Untuk artikel pertama, kita akan membahas terlebih dahulu mengenai penyebutan anggota keluarga, atau yang dalam bahasa Inggrisnya kita sebut dengan “family members”. Dalam “family members”, kita mengenal adanya keluarga langsung, keluarga “tetanggaan” atau tidak langsung, dan keluarga khusus.

Masing-masing kosa kata bahasa Inggris untuk anggota pada kelompok “family members” tersebut dan contoh-contoh penggunaannya dalam kalimat akan dipaparkan pada uraian poin berikut ini:

1. Berbagai Macam Vocabulary untuk Keluarga Langsung

Keluarga langsung yang dimaksud adalah keluarga yang garis pada pohon keluarganya langsung tersambung dengan kita. Dari sini, kita mengenal sebutan “parents” yang berarti “orang tua (kandung)” kita dan “siblings” yang berarti “saudara (kandung) kita”, di mana keduanya termasuk vocabulary untuk keluarga langsung.

Vocabulary yang dapat kita pelajari dari “parents” adalah “father” yang artinya “ayah” atau “mother” yang artinya “ibu”. Adapun contoh kalimat berikut ini mengilustrasikan penggunaan vocabulary “parents”, “father”, dan “mother”:

My father is a policeman (Ayah saya adalah seorang polisi).My mother is a homemaker (Ibu saya adalah ibu rumah tangga).Our parents are not here (Orang tua kami tidak di sini).

Sementara itu, vocabulary “siblings” mencakup “brother” yang artinya saudara laki-laki dan “sister” yang artinya saudara perempuan. Bagaimana dengan contoh-contoh kalimatnya? Yuk, kita lihat saja poin-poin berikut ini:

I have one brother and one sister (Saya punya satu saudara laki-laki dan satu saudara perempuan).My brother is six years old now (Saudara laki-laki saya sekarang usianya 6 tahun).Their siblings are currently high school students (Saudara-saudara mereka sekarang adalah murid SMA).

34. answer in your opinion! Can you please mention who relatives (family members) are?​


Jawaban:

In my opinion, family members or relatives typically include individuals who are connected by blood relations, marriage, or legal adoption. Here are some common examples of family members or relatives:

1. Parents: Mother and father who are responsible for giving birth to or raising a child.

2. Siblings: Brothers and sisters who share at least one common parent.

3. Grandparents: The parents of one's parents.

4. Grandchildren: The children of one's children.

5. Aunts and Uncles: Siblings of one's parents.

6. Nieces and Nephews: Children of one's siblings.

7. Cousins: Children of one's aunts and uncles.

8. Spouse: Husband or wife in a legally recognized marriage or partnership.

9. In-Laws: Relatives through marriage, including parents-in-law, siblings-in-law, and children-in-law.

10. Children: Sons and daughters of an individual.

11. Stepfamily Members: Relatives related through remarriage, such as step-parents, step-siblings, and step-children.

12. Extended Family: Relatives beyond immediate family, including great-grandparents, great-aunts/uncles, and second cousins.

It is important to note that family structures and definitions may vary across cultures, and the specific members considered as family can differ from person to person based on individual circumstances and relationships.

Penjelasan:

In general, relatives, also known as family members, typically include individuals who are connected through blood or marriage and share a common ancestry or legal bond. The specific individuals considered as relatives may vary depending on cultural, societal, and legal norms. However, I can provide a general overview of common relatives:

1. Parents: Mother and father are considered immediate relatives. They are usually the individuals who have biologically or legally given birth to or adopted a person.

2. Siblings: Brothers and sisters are siblings. They share at least one biological or adoptive parent with an individual.

3. Grandparents: Grandfathers and grandmothers are the parents of a person's parents.

4. Aunts and Uncles: These are the siblings of a person's parents. An aunt is the sister of one's mother or father, while an uncle is the brother of one's mother or father.

5. Cousins: Cousins are the children of one's aunts or uncles. They are considered to be in the same generation as oneself.

6. Spouse: A husband or wife is a person's partner in marriage.

7. Children: Sons and daughters are the offspring of individuals.

8. Grandchildren: The children of a person's children.

These are just some of the common relatives, and the specific terminology and relationships may vary across cultures and individual family structures. It's important to note that family structures can be diverse and may include additional relatives such as in-laws, stepfamily members, half-siblings, and extended family members beyond immediate relatives.
