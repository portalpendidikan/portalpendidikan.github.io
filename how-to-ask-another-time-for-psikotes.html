6. buatlah satu dialog (b.Iggris) yang ada give information, How to give opinion , how to ask for clarity!​


Jawaban:

Contoh dialog 1 :

Kinan: Kania, please help me choose the best dress to wear tonight.

Kania: Okay. What is the occasion?

Kinan: It’s my best friend’s birthday party.

Kania: Oh, I see.

Kinan: What do you think about this one?

Kania: Uhmm.. I think it’s too bright. Do you have another color?

Kinan: What about this one?

Kania: Oh, that’s nice. It suits your height and skin color.

Kinan: Thank you, Kania.

Terjemahan dialog 1 :

Kinan: Kak Kania, tolong bantu aku memilih gaun yang paling bagus untuk dikenakan malam ini.

Kania: Oke. Apa acaranya?

Kinan: Pesta ulang tahun sahabatku.

Kania: Oh, begitu.

Kinan: Bagaimana pendapatmu dengan yang ini?

Kania: Uhmm... Menurutku itu terlalu cerah. Apa kamu punya warna lain?

Kinan: Bagaimana dengan yang ini?

Kania: Oh, bagus sekali. Pas dengan tinggi dan warna kulitmu.

Kinan: Terima kasih, Kak Kania.

Contoh dialog 2 :

Haikal: Hi, guys! Have you heard about the new rules in our school?

Dirga: Yeah.

Haikal: What do you think?

Dirga: Personally I think that arriving at school before 6.30 is too early. We don’t have time to have breakfast.

Jerry: I don’t think it’s bad. We can wake up and go to school earlier.

Haikal: Yeah, I see your point, Jerry. But, it will extend our school hours. In my point of view, studying in such a long hours is ineffective.

Dirga: Yeah, I heard that some student council members would talk to the teachers so that they could reconsider that point.

Jerry: Uhmm.. I see. For me, it doesn’t matter if we should arrive at school before 6.30, but if you guys have any objection to the rules, maybe it is better if the rules are changed as before.

Terjemahan dialog 2 :

Haikal: Hai, teman-teman! Apa kalian sudah mendengar tentang peraturan baru di sekolah kita?

Dirga: Iya.

Haikal: Bagaimana menurutmu?

Dirga: Secara pribadi menurutku tiba di sekolah sebelum pukul 6.30 itu terlalu dini. Kita tidak punya waktu untuk sarapan.

Jerry: Menurutku itu tidak buruk. Kita bisa bangun dan pergi ke sekolah lebih awal.

Haikal: Ya, aku mengerti maksudmu, Jerry. Tapi, itu akan memperpanjang jam sekolah kita. Menurutku, belajar dalam waktu yang lama itu tidak efektif.

Dirga: Ya, aku mendengar bahwa beberapa anggota OSIS akan berbicara dengan para guru sehingga mereka bisa mempertimbangkan kembali hal itu.

Jerry: Uhmm .. Begitu. Bagiku tidak masalah jika kita harus tiba di sekolah sebelum jam 6.30, tapi jika kalian keberatan dengan aturan itu, mungkin lebih baik jika aturannya diubah seperti sebelumnya.

Contoh dialog 3 :

Vio: Good afternoon, friends. Let's discuss straight to the point. Today I got three options about the closing event. The first one is a mini concert, the second one is a color run, and the last is a costume party. What do you guys think about these options?

Lana: I think the second option is fun. Many people will join that kind of event.

Sheila: Well, I don’t think everyone like that kind of activity. Maybe a mini concert is the best option. We can invite one or two guests to attract the audience.

Dirga: Yeah, some people is not really into sport activity. Okay, what about you, Fikri?

Fikri: In my opinion, costume party is the best choice. Why? Because we don’t have to raise large funds. It’s a simple one, I guess.

Dirga: Oh, that’s right. Lana and Sheila, what do you think about this? Do you agree with Fikri’s opinion?

Lana: Yeah, I think it’s also fun.

Sheila: That’s a reasonable opinion. So, yeah, I agree with you, Fikri.

Dirga: Okay. Now we’ve decided the closing event. Tomorrow we’ll talk about the details

Terjemahan dialog 3 :

Vio: Selamat siang, teman-teman. Mari langsung membicarakan intinya. Hari ini aku mendapat tiga opsi tentang acara penutupan. Yang pertama adalah konser mini, yang kedua adalah color run, dan yang terakhir adalah pesta kostum. Apa pendapat kalian tentang opsi ini?

Lana: Menurutku pilihan kedua menyenangkan. Banyak orang akan mengikuti acara semacam itu.

Sheila: Menurutku tidak semua orang menyukai kegiatan seperti itu. Mungkin konser mini adalah pilihan terbaik. Kita bisa mengundang satu atau dua tamu untuk menarik penonton.

Dirga: Ya, sebagian orang tidak terlalu suka olahraga. Oke, bagaimana denganmu, Fikri?

Fikri: Menuruku, pesta kostum adalah pilihan terbaik. Mengapa? Karena kita tidak perlu mengumpulkan dana yang besar. Itu sederhana, aku rasa.

Dirga: Oh, benar. Lana dan Sheila, bagaimana menurut kalian tentang ini? Apakah kalian setuju dengan pendapat Fikri?

Lana: Ya, menurutku itu juga menyenangkan.

Sheila: Itu pendapat yang masuk akal. Jadi, ya, aku setuju denganmu, Fikri.

Dirga: Oke. Sekarang kita sudah memutuskan acara penutupannya. Besok kita akan membahas detailnya.


7. Do you know how to ask for attention in spoken andwritten forms? - Brainly #Backtoschool2019


how to ask for attention in spoken andwritten forms:

Excuse me! Look! Hey! Hello! Attention, please! Can I get the attention! Thanks. Stop talking please. Everybody, may I have your attention, please ? Can I have your attention, please?Pembahasan

Asking attention adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk menarik atau meminta perhatian kepada orang lain dengan tujuan agar orang tersebut memperhatikan kita.

Beberapa contoh ungkapan asking for attention adalah:

Excuse me! Listen to me! Look at me! Look here. Look! Hey! Hello! Attention, please! Can I get the attention! Thanks. Stop talking please… Everybody, may I have your attention, please ? Can I have your attention, please? Excuse me, I wonder if I could trouble you …. May I have your attention, please? Sorry to trouble you. Sorry to bother you. Look what I’ve got here.

Sedangkan Giving attention adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk memberikan perhatian atau atensi kepada orang lain yang sedang berbicara. Beberapa ungkapan giving attention yang sering digunakan dalam bahasa Inggris adalah:

I see. Tell me more about it. Really? Mmm… What happens next? And then what? What’s next? Is that all? That’s alright I see. Tell me more about it. Oh, yes. Really? Mmm… Oh, my God! What happens next? A ha… And then what? How interesting! What’s next? I know what you mean. Is that all? Oh, oh! Indeed? Well, well, well… Oh, no!

Contoh percakapan asking and giving attention:

Stranger: Excuse me, can I ask you something?

Rina: oh yes, what can I help for you?

Stranger: Do you know where the post office is?

RIna: I see. You can go straight from here then turn right on the crossjunction. The post office is on the left side of the street.

Stranger: Thank you very much.

Rina: You're welcome.

9. Why are we reluctant to ask many questions to someone we meet for the first time?​


Jawaban:

The thing about asking many questions to someone we meet for the first time is that our question may offend them and make them feel uncomfortable, especially if the questions are about private matters. We do not know much about their life and their struggles, that is why we do not want to offend them with our questions.
