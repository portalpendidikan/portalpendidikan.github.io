Jawaban:

Pengertian At Least

Kata ini berarti no less than dan digunakan ketika kita ingin membicarakan jumlah minimum yang diharapkan. Selain itu, ada beberapa kegunaan-nya dalam kalimat Bahasa Inggris. Di antaranya adalah:

Mengulangi apa yang telah kita katakan untuk menegaskannya.

Digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu hal yang positif di dalam situasi yang tampaknya negatif.

Kata ini juga dapat ditambahkan adverb of degree “very” menjadi at the very least untuk memberi penekanan lebih.

Untuk lebih jelasnya, kamu bisa melihat beberapa contoh kalimat di bawah ini:

The prime minister advised that each family should have at least three children.

He doesn’t have enough experience in marketing and sales. At least, I think he’s not experienced enough.

At least he came to your party even if he was late.

Fungsinya dalam Kalimat Bahasa Inggris

Dari kegunaan dan juga definisi mengenai at least, mari kita bahas lebih jauh mengenai fungsi dan juga contoh kalimatnya.

Menyatakan jumlah minimum dan malah bisa jadi lebih banyak

You should have at least Rp 500.000 if you want to open a bank account.

The applicants need to have at least five years of working experience as a manager.

To be the best graduate student, at least you should get a score of eight in all subjects.

Mr. Gray will remain the director for three years at least.

The disease killed at least 120 people in England last year.

We are expecting to at least double our money.

Aim to have at least half a pint of milk each day.

About two-thirds of adults consult their doctor at least once a year.

You should use this mouthwash several times a day – twice at the very least.

Menyatakan tidak ada yang terjadi atau sebuah fakta

At least no one can accuse me of hiding the truth.

You might at least have waited for me.

We have reached an agreement that at least gives the company a chance to survive

How to write compliment is as follows :

1. Possessive Pronouns (your, her, his, their) / Personal Pronouns (you, he, she, it, they) + is / are / look (s) / sound (s) + Adjective

Example : Your new hair looks cool!

2. Personal Pronouns (I, We) + like / love + noun phrase

Example : I like your drawings. They look real.

3. This / That + is + (such) + a / an + (really) + adjective + noun phrase

Example: This is such an amazing photo.

4. What + a / an + adjective + noun phrase

Example: What a great idea!
